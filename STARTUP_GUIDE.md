## Technical Details (For Reference)

- **Backend**: Runs on port `8080` (FastAPI)
- **Frontend**: Runs on port `3000` (Python SimpleHTTP)
- **Database**: `backend/rvsync.db` (Auto-created)

To stop the platform, just close the two minimized command windows that open up.
